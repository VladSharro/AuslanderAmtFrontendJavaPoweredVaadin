Guide

If you come to Germany to study and live here, you will have to go through some bureaucratic and administrative steps along the way. Which pieces of paperwork you need depends on your nationality.

Three Essential Steps

  1. Visa: The permission to enter Germany for a defined purpose and a certain amount of time. Before that time runs out, you will have to apply for a residence permit if you need to stay longer.
  2. Residence Registration: In Germany, it is compulsory for all people to register with the local authorities where they live, as soon as they have moved into their accommodation. Learn more about residence registration.
  3. Residence Permit: The permission to live in Germany for a specified amount of time. Get detailed information about residence permits.
